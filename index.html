Taipei Introduction

Taipei, the capital Taiwan, is a modern city located on the Northern most part in Taiwan. The skyline is crowned by the 509m tall, bamboo-shaped Taipei 101 Skyscraper. Taipei os also known for its lively street food and many night markets
